Maneki-Neko Exhibit
Kids Entry is Free
Visit the Maneki-Neko exhibit in the Marchant Museum. For a short time only, see the work of authentic Japanese artists and local favorites mixed together in our latest attraction.
Tickets start at $14 for a single adult, $10 for senior citizens, and kids up to 10 years of age entry is free.
